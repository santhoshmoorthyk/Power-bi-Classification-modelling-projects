Loan Approval Report
Overview

This report focuses on analyzing loan applications and their approval status it includes multiple visuals to understand the distribution of credit score approval rate client points loan amount income and risk category across cities and overall the report is designed to help understand which clients are approved rejected and how different factors affect approval

Features

Cards for total applications approval count rejected loans and approval rate

Slicers for loan approval risk category and city

Stacked bar chart of credit score ranges with approval rate

Top 5 clients by points and their loan income ratio

Line and cluster column chart showing average income and average loan amount by approval

Clustered bar chart of risk category against loan approved and rejected

Stacked column chart showing approved loans by city only for cities with approved loans

Data Preparation

Data includes applicant name city credit score income loan amount years employed points and loan approved status basic transformations and calculated columns were performed such as risk category points ratio and credit score band before creating visuals

Purpose

This report is a basic level classification dashboard that categorizes loan applications based on applicant financial attributes risk and approval status using Power BI and DAX it helps visualize approval patterns trends risk assessment and city wise performance
